How to install
1
Tap Download above
Your phone downloads NOVA.apk from GitHub. No account needed.
2
Allow install from browser
Android asks "Allow installs from this source?" — tap Settings and enable it. One-time only.
Settings → Install unknown apps → your browser → Allow
3
Tap Install
Open the downloaded file. Tap Install. NOVA appears on your home screen.
4
Enable Accessibility Service
Required for navigation commands — back, home, scroll, screenshot.
Settings → Accessibility → NOVA Voice Controller → Enable
5
Train your wake word
In the app: Set Wake Word → say your chosen word 20 times → Train. NOVA learns your voice entirely on your phone. Then say your word — NOVA replies "Yes?"
